Orchvate offers an innovative approach to build a neurodiverse and inclusive society, where boundaries are broken and we collaborate to create wonders. At Orchvate we are a neurodiverse team of psychologists (clinical & organizational) and neurodivergents who have chosen to unmask and self-advocate for the cause. We have the professional expertise to deal with neuro-divergents, recognize their strengths, match them to the right roles and integrate them into the workplace because we understand the challenges they are likely to face. Alongside, we have the professional expertise to partner with enterprises, understand hiring requirements, coach ND resources and enable right fits to achieve organizational goals. Orchvate best collaborates with both entities and bridges the gap to build the neuro-diverse workplace.
